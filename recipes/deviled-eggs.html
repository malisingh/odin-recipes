Everything Bagel Deviled Eggs

Everything bagel seasoning is so good on these deviled eggs. They are absolutely delicious. This recipe is easily multiplied, too, if you are serving a larger crowd.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs, hard boiled, peeled
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons everything bagel seasoning

Steps

  1. Slice eggs in half lengthwise, remove yolks, and place them in a bowl. Place egg whites aside.
  2. Mash egg yolks. Stir in mayonnaise and mustard until well combined. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Place egg yolk mixture in a piping bag and pipe mixture into egg whites. Sprinkle generously with Everything Bagel seasoning.
